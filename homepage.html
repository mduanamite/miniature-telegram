Welcome.

This website is my attempt to share my collection and my journey in the world of studio glass. For this scientist, who used clear borosilicate glass apparatus in the lab, studio glass, with its vibrant and often riotous color palette, was an exciting new world. I have enjoyed learning about the use of glass as an artistic medium, have marveled at the amazing works of art and have had the privilege to meet many wonderful people - artists, gallery owners and fellow collectors.
